★About Me ★

I may be a literal grill on the internet, but my name is Borealis Capps in the offline world. I'm an award-winning poet and author living with my three cats and partner in the Pacific Northwest. I'm also a certified hentai master having watched over 100 hentai shows and OVAs and read plenty of manga as well. I’ve been trying to increase scholarly discussion surrounding hentai in particular as I think it is often overlooked by other anime writers despite how interesting it can be to analyse. It’s also never been harder to get a publication to publish works digging into adult media of any kind with increasing attempts at censorship throughout the world and threats by payment processors.

With all that in mind, this place is my little way to fight back and get to write about the things I like for the lovely little weirdos like you who want to read about it!

On the less hornyside of things, I'm queer, disabled, and a fierce fighter for all marginalized people. While all those adventures above sound exciting, they also included a stint with homelessness. All of these things have informed how I act today. To put it simply: If you lean towards the right you won't like my content or me so kindly escort yourself elsewhere.
